BCH Submission Strategy — Which Plan to Lead With

ARDA Plans (98 units) delivers $5.7M more to Al over 20 years — this is the recommended lead plan for BCH submission given the larger grant, Heal House community impact, and superior total return.

Concept Plan (72 units) offers a cleaner, faster path — stronger DSCR (2.0x vs. 1.79x), simpler construction (single 6-storey vs. mixed-height), and lower total cost. Best option if speed-to-market or BCH qualification risk is the priority concern.

Recommendation: Submit the ARDA Plans as the primary application, with the Concept Plan held as a fallback if BCH requires a simplified scope. Both are BCH-viable. The additional $7.1M in grant funding under ARDA makes it the financially superior choice.
